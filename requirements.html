Get In The Game

HOW TO JOIN

Everything you need to compete in MashUp Golf.

Prerequisites

WHAT YOU'LL NEED

01
Access to a Golf Simulator Running GSPRO

You'll need a physical golf simulator setup running GSPRO software. GSPRO is the simulation platform the entire league plays on — all shot data, course play, and scoring runs through it.

02
Launch Monitor Connected to GSPRO

An official launch monitor with a live connection to GSPRO is required. This ensures consistent, verified ball data across the entire field.

03
Active SGT Account

You'll need an active account on the Simulator Golf Tour platform throughout the entire season.

04
Active COMBO Handicap — Minimum 20 Rounds

Your SGT COMBO handicap needs at least 20 rounds posted before the season begins. No cramming — don't try to post a bunch of rounds right before the season starts. The admin team monitors handicap activity.

05
Ability to Live Stream on YouTube

Every round must be streamed live on YouTube and the link shared in Discord before you tee off. See the streaming section below for the full details.

06
Full Season Payment Before Week 1

Payment for the full season is due before your first event. Reach out to the admin team for details each season.

Fairness First

HANDICAPPING

We use SGT's COMBO handicap system, which looks at up to 48 rounds from the last 6 months. How many of those rounds count toward your handicap depends on how many you've logged — the more rounds you have, the more scores factor in. You can find the full breakdown on the SGT handicap page. On top of that, if you're newer to the platform, a small adjustment is applied to keep the competition fair:

Rounds Logged
Adjustment
20 – 35 rounds
−2 strokes
36 – 47 rounds
−1 stroke
48+ rounds
No adjustment

Each week, the field is also normalized so the best player in the field plays to scratch (0 handicap), with everyone else adjusting relative to them. This keeps the competition balanced regardless of skill level.

Verification

STREAMING REQUIREMENTS

Streaming is how we keep the league honest and accountable. Every player is required to go live on YouTube before their round starts and share the link in the league's Discord channel.

Your stream must clearly show:
▸ The ball clearly visible in the impact zone throughout the round
▸ Your full GSPRO screen at all times
▸ Minimum data tiles: Ball Speed, Back Spin, Side Spin, Spin Axis, HLA, and VLA
For team events, you have two options:
A. One team member streams the entire round with all players visible, or
B. Each player streams their own round individually.
Good to Know

If you forget to start your stream, you can replay the round — but your worst score will count. Technical issues are reviewed by the admin team case by case. Rounds played without a valid stream, or with missing data tiles, may be disqualified from that week's competition.
