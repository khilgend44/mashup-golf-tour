What We Are

WHAT IS MASHUP GOLF?

MashUp Golf is a weekly simulator golf league that brings the energy of a real competitive tour to the sim. Each season features a rotating lineup of formats — team scrambles, ringer contests, shambles, escalators, and more — so every week feels like a new challenge. Whether you're a scratch player or carrying a handicap, there's always a format that will test a different part of your game.

The Name

WHY "MASHUP"?

The name says it all. We take all the formats we love about golf — best ball, scramble, stroke play, match play — and mash them into one season. The variety keeps things exciting, rewards different skills each week, and makes sure no single player dominates just by hitting it long. Fresh challenge, every week.

Culture

OUR COMMUNITY

MashUp Golf is built on good competition, good banter, and mutual respect. We're a tight-knit group of sim golfers who take the game seriously — without taking ourselves too seriously.

Golf is a gentleman's game, and we hold that standard here. Players are expected to treat each other with civility on stream, in Discord, and on the virtual course. Compete hard, have fun, and represent the game well. Poor sportsmanship or disrespectful behavior may result in exclusion from future seasons.
